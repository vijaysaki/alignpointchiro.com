About

Meet Dr. Kristian Esteve, D.C. (Dr. K)

Dr. Kristian Esteve, D.C. — known to his patients as Dr. K — is a Lexington-based chiropractor dedicated to helping patients relieve pain, restore mobility, and improve their quality of life through personalized, evidence-based chiropractic care.

Inspired by his own life-changing recovery through chiropractic treatment, Dr. K brings a unique blend of clinical precision, modern technology, and genuine compassion to every patient visit. At AlignPoint Chiropractic, he focuses on identifying the root cause of pain—not just treating symptoms.

He performs thorough examinations and utilizes digital X-ray imaging when appropriate to develop targeted, individualized treatment plans designed for long-term results. His clinical focus includes care for neck pain, low back pain, sciatica, headaches, postural imbalances, sports injuries, and personal injury cases.

Patients appreciate Dr. K’s clear communication, detailed explanations, and patient-centered approach. His mission is simple: help patients feel better, move better, and live healthier—naturally.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequency depends on your goals, exam findings, and how your body responds. We’ll recommend a plan and adjust it as you improve.

Many patients seek supportive care for certain headache patterns. We’ll evaluate your history and determine whether chiropractic care is appropriate.

Safety depends on your exam findings and whether you’re an appropriate candidate. Dr. K will discuss options and recommend what fits your case.

Posture is influenced by joint mobility, muscle balance, habits, and ergonomics. We can address mobility and guide exercises and workstation changes.
