Pain descriptions we commonly hear

Sharp, aching, cramping, spasms, dull, shooting, stiffness, throbbing, burning, swelling, numbness, tingling, stabbing — and trouble with sitting, bending, standing, lying down, walking, running. Pain that travels or comes and goes (acute or chronic).
