What to Expect

Your First Visits at AlignPoint

First Appointment: Consultation & Exam

Your first visit is all about getting to know you and understanding what’s going on. During this visit, we will:

  • Review your health history
  • Discuss your concerns and goals
  • Perform a chiropractic exam
  • Take any necessary imaging

This helps determine the best path forward and whether chiropractic care is right for you.

Second Appointment: Findings & First Adjustment

At your next visit, we’ll walk you through what we found and what it means for your care. This visit includes:

  • Reviewing exam and imaging results
  • Explaining your personalized care plan
  • Your first adjustment, if appropriate

Everything is explained clearly so you feel confident and comfortable moving forward.

Schedule Now

After Your First Adjustment

Everyone responds differently after their first adjustment — and these experiences are completely normal. You may notice one of three things:

  1. You feel sore* — common as joints and muscles start moving better (like starting a new workout).
  2. You feel great — early relief can be temporary; consistent care helps stability over time.
  3. You feel no big change — some bodies need more time to adapt and heal.

*If You Feel Sore

  • Warm shower or bath
  • Heating pad or rice pack
  • Apply heat 20 minutes on / 20 minutes off as needed
  • Icing 20 minutes before bed (if helpful)

A Gentle Reminder

Healing takes time. Every visit builds on the last, and your body adapts at its own pace. We’re here to support you along the way.
